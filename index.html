ABOUT

Thank you for visiting the Langley RoadRiders website. Originally formed in the 1980’s as a small group of riders, the Langley RoadRiders Motorcycle Club welcomes you to join one of BC’s longest actively engaged motorcycle clubs! .

We are a friendly group of motorcyclists of all ages and owners of many different types of motorcycles. We ride twice a week for no particular reason except for the love of riding and being with good company. Most long weekends we organize are three-day rides or longer. Please consult our ride schedule.

Club events like BBQ's and the Christmas party are reserved for members of Langley RoadRiders only.

JOIN US

JOIN US

Having a good ride is just part of the fun. Whether it’s a get-together breakfast before we blast off, or mid-day lunch break on a hot sunny day, nothing could be better than sitting down with fellow riders, sharing a few laughs, exchanging ride stories and making new and lasting friends. Your membership in Langley RoadRiders is filled with a ton of fun activities! Most importantly, it’s made up of frequent, well-organized and safety conscious road trips. If you’d like to meet the members and find out more about the club, come join a monthly meeting or contact us HERE

(Monthly Meetings are held on the 4th Thursday of each month at Ricky’s All Day Grill in Cloverdale at 17830 56 Avenue Cloverdale BC, meeting starts @ 6:30PM with the room open at 5:30PM to those who want to have dinner together beforehand.)

To recieve a membership application package please contact us through the CONTACT page of this website.

SCHEDULE

Tuesday Night Rides - The Langley RoadRiders ride every Tuesday night. The starting location is McDonald’s @ 19780 Fraser Hwy, Langley, BC Please come with a full tank and time to sign a waiver if you haven’t already. Kickstands up at 7:00pm The Volunteer Ride Leader chooses the route at the end of which we stop for coffee and a few laughs then disperse to home from there.

Sunday Rides - The Langley RoadRiders ride every Sunday. Please ensure you have proper identification for all US Rides. The starting location for Sunday Rides is McDonald’s @ 19780 Fraser Hwy, Langley, BC Please come with a full tank and time to sign a waiver if you haven’t already. Kickstands up at 8:00am (or as noted on the Ride Schedule) The Sunday rides usually have a lunch stop as noted on the ride schedule.
